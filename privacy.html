Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how XUQI Associates collects, uses, and protects any personal information you provide when you use our website or submit an enquiry.

We are committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected and that all personal data is handled in accordance with UK GDPR regulations.

  • Your name and contact details (email, phone number)
  • Project information you choose to provide
  • Location details such as postcode or address
  • Technical data such as IP address and browser type

How we use your information

  • Respond to enquiries and provide quotations
  • Deliver architectural and structural engineering services
  • Maintain internal project records
  • Improve our services and communication
  • Send relevant updates (only where consent is given)

Data security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data against unauthorised access, disclosure, or loss.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data, and to withdraw consent for communications at any time.

To exercise your rights, contact us at xuqiassociates@outlook.com.

Data retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary for legal, operational, and service purposes.
