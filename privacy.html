Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how XUQI Associates collects, uses, and protects any personal information you provide when you use our website or submit an enquiry.

We are committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected and that all personal data is handled in accordance with UK GDPR regulations.

Information we may collect

  • Your name
  • Your contact information (including email address and phone number)
  • Project details you choose to share with us
  • Location information (such as address or postcode)
  • Technical data such as IP address and browser type

How we use your information

  • Respond to your enquiries and provide quotations
  • Deliver architectural and structural engineering services
  • Maintain internal project records
  • Improve our services and communication
  • Send relevant updates (only if you opt in)

Data security

We are committed to ensuring your information is secure. Appropriate technical and organisational measures are in place to prevent unauthorised access, disclosure, or loss of data.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies to improve user experience and analyse website performance. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

External links

Our website may contain links to other websites. Once you leave our site, we are not responsible for the privacy practices of external websites.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. You may also withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time.

To exercise your rights, please contact us at info@xuqiassociates.com.

Data retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected, including legal and operational requirements.
