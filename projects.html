Our Projects

Residential Loft Extension – Norwich

Full architectural and structural design for a residential loft extension in Norwich, delivered from concept to technical approval. The scope included pre-application advice, planning submission, building regulations design, and structural calculations. A coordinated approach ensured a sensitive response to the existing building while delivering a practical and well-integrated extension.

Modern Office Fit-Out – Cambridge Business Park

Survey, assessment, and design support for an open-plan office renovation. The scope included evaluating the existing structural arrangement and providing design solutions to support a more flexible open-plan layout. Floor load capacity was optimised through targeted structural interventions, ensuring improved performance and adaptability to the revised spatial configuration.

Heritage Building Refurbishment – Ledbury

Assessment and strengthening of a Grade II listed building. Implemented minimal intervention techniques to maintain historical features while improving structural integrity.

Bungalow Alteration – London

Integrated architectural and structural design for the alteration of an existing bungalow in London. The intervention refined the internal layout to create a more connected and adaptable living environment, improving spatial efficiency while maintaining structural continuity across the existing fabric.

Structural Crack Assessment – Barnet

Structural investigation and assessment of observed cracking in a residential property in Barnet. The project involved identifying potential causes of structural movement, evaluating building safety, and providing repair recommendations in accordance with UK Building Regulations and best engineering practice.

Rear Extension – St Albans

Integrated architectural and structural design for a rear extension to an existing residential property in St Albans. The design rationalised the ground floor arrangement to improve spatial flow and expand usable living areas, while ensuring efficient load transfer through the new structural configuration.
