About Us

Based in Cambridge, XUQI Associates delivers smart, reliable structural engineering solutions for homes, businesses, and public projects. We combine technical expertise with practical insight to make sure every project is safe, efficient, and built to last.

From surveys and assessments to design and planning support, we guide our clients through every step with professionalism and care. At XUQI Associates, quality and safety come first—helping Cambridge and the surrounding areas build with confidence.

Our Projects

  • Riverside Residential Extension – Cambridge
    Complete structural design and assessment for a two-storey extension to a Victorian townhouse. Delivered safe load-bearing solutions while preserving the character of the original building.
  • Modern Office Fit-Out – Cambridge Business Park
    Structural survey and reinforcement design for an open-plan office renovation. Optimized floor load capacities and ensured compliance with current building regulations.
  • Heritage Building Refurbishment – Central Cambridge
    Assessment and strengthening of a Grade II listed building. Implemented minimal intervention techniques to maintain historical features while improving structural integrity.
  • Loft Conversion – Suburban Cambridge
    Structural calculations and support design for a loft conversion. Maximized usable space while ensuring full compliance with Building Control.
  • Sustainable New Build – Cambridge Outskirts
    Full structural design for a low-carbon, energy-efficient home. Integrated steel and timber solutions to reduce environmental impact without compromising safety.
